Things that will help you to improve the contest performance.
1. Set a target. Like "I need to solve two problems during the contest." Increase the target eventually.
2. Learn more concepts. Knowing more concepts = the probability of solving more problems will increase.
3. Don't discuss during contests. Don't share hints or anything. Let others think for themselves. Discuss after the contest ends.
4. Upsolve after the contest.
5. Helping others or cheating during a contest won't help you in the long run.
6. Practice regularly. Consistency is key. Solve at least a few problems every day, even outside of contests.
7. Manage your time wisely. During a contest, don't get stuck on one problem for too long. Move to the next one and come back later.
8. Learn from others' solutions. After a contest, read the editorial and top participants' code. You will often discover cleaner and more efficient approaches.
